Custom Group Adventures for Summer Camps

Planning summer camp travel is time consuming. Juggling budgets, itineraries, and multiple vendors requires patience, knowledge of each location, and trusted connections. As a certified travel planner and former teen travel director for over 20 years, I know first-hand what goes into planning. Let me take one thing off your plate while you prepare for the upcoming summer.

Start a camp trip inquiry
No planning fee for camps – my commission is paid by the vendors.

How this works

Share your camp dates, age groups, budget range, and a rough idea of where you’d like to go. I’ll build options, compare vendors, and help you lock in a trip that fits your schedule and risk tolerance.

What we can book

Accommodations

From luxury resorts and villas to value-driven hotels, I work with properties that understand camp groups, rooming lists, curfews, and supervision needs – and help negotiate group rates and inclusions.

Activities & Tours

Private city tours, hands-on museums, national parks, adventure parks, and more. Whether you’re in DC, Tennessee, or beyond, I’ll build age-appropriate, high-impact itineraries that keep counselors and parents comfortable.

Cruises

Five-day Bahamas cruise with 50 teens? We’ll plan cabin blocks, private events on board, and the logistics before and after the sailing so the experience is smooth from bus to ship and back.

All-Inclusive Resorts

Beach Olympics, snorkeling, and large-group meals all under one roof. I’ll match your camp’s budget and risk profile to the right resort partners and help secure the dates you need.
