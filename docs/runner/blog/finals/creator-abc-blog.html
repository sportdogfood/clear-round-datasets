WEC Ocala Spring 2026

In the heart of Florida 's horse country, the World Equestrian Center in Ocala welcomes back its Spring Series for another season of high-caliber hunter and jumper competition.

With the World Equestrian Center continuing to raise the bar for excellence, the Spring Series remains a cornerstone event on the Florida equestrian calendar.

Stay

Competitors and guests enjoy premium lodging just steps from the arenas. The Equestrian Hotel and The Riding Academy Hotel provide elegant on-site stays, while Ocala RV Camp Resort offers a relaxed extended-option nearby. Other nearby hotels include Springhill Suites, Holiday Inn Express, and Hilton Ocala, each within an easy drive.

Dine

Dining options at and around WEC cater to every taste. On-site favorites include Stirrups Restaurant for refined dinines and Emma's Patisserie for coffee and pastries. Beyond the venue, Darrell's Diner and Ipanema Brazilian Steak House also stand out with local flavors, while popular spots like Romas Italian, Marco's Pizza, and Jersey Mike Subs provide quick meals.

Essentials

Essential stops are close at hand for competitors and visitors. Walgreens and CVS answer basic needs, while Contour Saddlery, Tack Shack, TT Distributors, and Seminole Feed Stores supply equestrian gear and feed. Publix offers convenient grocery and supplies from boutiques like Exceptional Equestrian and Ocala Breeders Feed & Supply.

From exceptional amenities to world-class arenas, WEC Ocala continues to set the standard for equestrian sport in Florida. The Spring Series offers an atmosphere that balances competition with community, making it a highlight on every rider's calendar.
